\subsection{CT12: Well-founded structural peeling}
\label{sec:ct12-automation}

CT12 is the unique cyclic machine. Load zero yields the exhausted certificate; positive load is peeled and the first declared restoration is selected. A demand or tier restoration terminates, while a continuation carries its own strict load decrease and is the only edge permitted to re-enter saturation. The result also records iteration and trace-length bounds.

A theorem instantiation supplies the problem-specific static entries in the first table. The framework constructs every remaining node output, exhaustive decision, certificate, residual, edge, and trace theorem.

\paragraph{Public automation surface.}
\texttt{StructuralExhaustion.CT12.run} returns the evidence-carrying \texttt{ExecutionResult}. \texttt{ct12\_\allowbreak{}execute}, \texttt{ct12}, and \texttt{ct12\_\allowbreak{}total} expose execution, semantic soundness, and totality, respectively.
