# Repair a Hypostructure Manuscript

Freeze the failed claim and recover its exact incoming residual and accumulated ledger. Normalize the quantifiers and identify which terminal was omitted or incorrectly closed.

Repair the proof by composing an existing reusable strategy or designing a new generic strategy with exhaustive terminals and a strict progress argument. Put missing generic machinery in Core and domain semantics in Graph/PDE.

Do not alter the target, add assumptions, reconstruct payloads, close a residual with an application theorem, or call a finalizer. Add the registered continuation to the DAG and inspect the automatic `problemDefinition.report`.
