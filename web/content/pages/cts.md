# CT chooser

CT1–CT17 are executable tactic contracts. Choose by matching the mathematical split to every computed terminal and complementary residual, then supply the smallest capability that makes the finite reference execution possible.

## Choosing safely

Start from the exact residual you must consume. Identify the finite schedule, response table, budget, rank, code, or peeling process already owned by that residual. A CT fits only when all its outcomes have meaningful consumers or closures.

## Shared execution shape

Each CT separates specification, capability, state, search, execution, and theorems. The public executor computes a typed trace and exact check count while retaining the literal predecessor in the accumulated stage.
